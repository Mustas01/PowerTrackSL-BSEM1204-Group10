About PowerTrack SL

Smart electricity monitoring and outage reporting platform for Sierra Leone.

Who We Are

PowerTrack SL is a digital platform designed to help communities across Sierra Leone report electricity outages and receive real-time updates about power restoration and maintenance.

Our mission is to improve communication between electricity providers and citizens by using modern web technologies.

Our Objectives

  • ⚡ Monitor electricity outages
  • ⚡ Provide outage updates
  • ⚡ Improve emergency reporting
  • ⚡ Promote energy-saving awareness
