About PowerTrack SL

Energy infrastructure remains one of the foundational cornerstones of socioeconomic development within Sierra Leone. However, a persistent challenge for domestic households and commercial enterprises alike has been the lack of real-time, centralized data regarding power distribution states and maintenance schedules. PowerTrack SL was conceptualized to solve this critical information asymmetric bottleneck. By engineering a dual-purpose data pipeline, the platform transforms ordinary citizens from passive consumers into active node reporters. Our architecture blends official grid operational inputs with structured, crowdsourced public fault reporting. The result is an intuitive, live dashboard interface that maps out grid stability, displays immediate appliance energy analytics, and delivers localized telemetry to keep the public safely informed.

Engineered to satisfy high-level UX requirements for the Web Design 1 Module framework at Limkokwing University.
