Strategic Energy Saving Recommendations

Simple optimizations to protect equipment from surges and scale down your prepaid EDSA token costs.

Install Surge Protectors & Delay Timers

Power restorations often carry high-voltage spikes. Use specialized delay switches for fridges and AC systems to prevent compressor burnouts.

Manage Thermal Mass Loadings

Keep your refrigerator well-filled (even with water bottles if empty). A full fridge retains cooling cycles significantly better during unexpected short blackouts.

Transition to Solar Auxiliaries

Shift high-frequency tasks like smartphone charging, outdoor compound wall lanterns, and fans to localized small solar power bricks to preserve your primary meter line.
